This dashboard analyzes customer purchasing behavior by summarizing key metrics and comparing patterns across customers, products, and transactions.

---

## Analysis Overview

- Calculated overall customer metrics, including total number of customers, average purchase amount, and average review rating, to understand customer engagement and satisfaction.

- Analyzed customer subscription status to compare purchasing behavior between subscribed and non-subscribed customers.

- Examined revenue distribution across product categories to identify categories contributing the most to overall revenue.

- Analyzed sales volume by product category to understand purchase frequency and category popularity.

- Segmented customers into age groups and compared their revenue contribution to identify high-value customer segments.

- Analyzed sales distribution across age groups to understand how purchase frequency varies by demographic.

- Reviewed discount application to calculate the percentage of purchases made with discounts for each product.

- Examined customer preferences across different shipping types to understand commonly used delivery options.

- Used interactive filters such as subscription status, gender, product category, and shipping type to explore and compare customer behavior across different segments.

---

## Summary Insight

- Customer purchasing behavior varies significantly across product categories and age groups.
- Discounts and subscriptions play an important role in influencing purchase decisions.
- Shipping preferences reflect customer convenience and buying patterns.
